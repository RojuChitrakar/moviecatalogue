After leaving the business one year earlier, battle-scarred stuntman 
        Colt Seavers springs back into action when the star of a big studio movie suddenly disappears.
         As the mystery surrounding the missing actor deepens, Colt soon finds himself ensnared in 
         a sinister plot that pushes him to the edge of a fall more dangerous than any stunt.
        Release date: 3 May 2024 (India)
        Director: David Leitch
        Box office: 10.48 crores USD
        Distributed by: Universal Pictures
        Based on: The Fall Guy; by Glen A. Larson
        Cinematography: Jonathan Sela
